DIABETES MELLITUS

Definition: Metabolic disorder causing high blood sugar.

Types:
1. Type 1 Diabetes:
   - Autoimmune
   - Usually childhood onset
   - Requires insulin

2. Type 2 Diabetes:
   - Insulin resistance
   - Most common type
   - Linked to obesity

Symptoms:
- Excessive thirst
- Frequent urination
- Unexplained weight loss
- Fatigue
- Blurred vision

Diagnosis:
- Fasting glucose >=126 mg/dL
- Random glucose >=200 mg/dL with symptoms
- HbA1c >=6.5%

Management:
- Blood sugar monitoring
- Healthy diet
- Regular exercise
- Medications
- Regular check-ups

Complications:
- Heart disease
- Nerve damage
- Kidney disease
- Eye damage
- Foot problems
