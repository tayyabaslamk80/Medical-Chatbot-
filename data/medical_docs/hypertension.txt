HYPERTENSION (High Blood Pressure)

Definition: Chronic condition with elevated blood pressure.

Classification:
- Normal: <120/80 mmHg
- Elevated: 120-129/<80 mmHg
- Stage 1: 130-139/80-89 mmHg
- Stage 2: >=140/90 mmHg

Symptoms:
Often no symptoms. When present: headaches, dizziness, nosebleeds.

Risk Factors:
- Age over 65
- Family history
- Obesity
- High salt diet
- Lack of exercise
- Smoking
- Excessive alcohol

Treatment:
1. Lifestyle changes:
   - DASH diet
   - Exercise 30 mins/day
   - Weight loss
   - Reduce salt
   - Limit alcohol
   
2. Medications:
   - ACE inhibitors
   - Beta blockers
   - Diuretics
   - Calcium channel blockers

Complications if untreated:
- Heart attack
- Stroke
- Kidney failure
- Vision loss
- Heart failure
